How fast can your locksmith Paris team arrive?

We dispatch the closest locksmith and keep you updated via SMS. Average arrival time is 30 minutes across central Paris and 40 minutes in the outer arrondissements.

Do you offer locksmith Paris fixed-price quotes?

Yes. We provide a clear fixed price covering travel, labor, and parts before we start. If we discover additional work is needed, we confirm and document it first.

Which lock brands do you work with?

We carry Fichet, Mul-T-Lock, Vachette, Abus, JPM, Winkhaus, and other leading brands, plus OEM cylinders for French and European doors.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cashless payments including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay, bank transfer, and instant payment links.

Do you verify customer identity before unlocking a door?

Yes. To protect property owners, we verify proof of address or ID before unlocking any door or handing over new keys.

Can you open my door without damage?

Our locksmiths use non-destructive techniques whenever possible. If drilling is unavoidable, we replace the cylinder immediately.

Do you guarantee your locksmith work?

Yes. All parts and workmanship include a 12-month warranty. Should an issue arise, contact us and we will revisit promptly.

Can you help with insurance claims after a break-in?

We produce insurance-compliant invoices, photos, and reports that you can submit to your insurer or property manager.

Is there an extra charge for late-night locksmith callouts?

We apply a modest night tariff between 10 pm and 6 am, which is fully disclosed in your quote before you confirm.

How should I prepare before your locksmith arrives?

Have identification ready, clear the door area, and if possible send photos of the lock mechanism so we arrive with the right parts.
